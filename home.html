Cool Calendar App for Clubs

At such a big school like UCLA it is hard to find a sense of community. My proposed application will strengthen the social fabric of student groups on college campuses and beyond by easily allowing members to create and view various social events to attend with their fellow members, as well as giving them an outlet to interact with one other.

This is my final project for DH150 Spring 2019
